Frequently Asked Questions

What's TravelBook?

TravelBook is a website built with Django framework. It allows you to find, filter and book their holidays
with a few clicks. In addition, through an authentication system can organize and save their searches.

How can I manage my bookings and favorites?

TravelBook allows saving of the rooms/locations in the user's personal page, accessible by clicking the username.
On this page you can show and delete your own reservations and favorite.

How can I vote my holiday?

TravelBook allows you to vote for your holiday by going to the personal page of each user.
In the pane on the reservation.

Can I see the position on map of locations?

TravelBook allows you to see the position of all locations on google maps.
By going to the Location List section.

How can I check if a room is booked or not, in the search?

It's very easy. In general:
Booked Room
Free Room
Favorite Room
Waiting list

Can I get on a waiting list if a reservation is deleted?

Sure, if you press the button "add to waiting list" in the search.
If and when an user deletes the reservation, you will be notified via email.

If i am an owner, can I add my hotel in TravelBook?

Sure, TravelBook allows owners to add their hotels.
In the personal page of each user section, there is an area where owners can add the hotel and related information.

Where do I sign?

TravelBook allows you to register by entering the login section. And by clicking "register."

How can I associate my user to a hotel owner?

If you send us your request, we give permission to your user needed to add location.
